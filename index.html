About Us

Learn Robo Nepal is a non-profit organization dedicated to promoting STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) education across Nepal. We believe that by equipping young minds with technical skills and innovative thinking, we can help build a brighter future for our country.

Our programs focus on hands-on learning experiences in robotics, programming, and emerging technologies. We work with high school students, providing them with the tools, knowledge, and mentorship they need to excel in the digital age.

Through workshops, competitions, and community events, we're creating a generation of problem-solvers and innovators who will drive Nepal's technological advancement.
